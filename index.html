gayatri mantra

ॐ भूर्भुवः स्वः तत्सवितुर्वरेण्यं । भर्गो देवस्य धीमहि धियो यो नः प्रचोदयात् ॥

Gayatri Mantra is the first mantra that is taught to Yogi’s, and is whispered in the child’s ear when he is born. It is the Mantra honouring the sun as the source of all life, and the source of the sun itself. It calls upon the energies of creation and asks to become like that; may we shine our light like the sun and serve all reality

Naga Rasta

“The more you meditate the more YOU realise jah!”

Many people know of the Rastafari’s popularised through Bob Marley, yet few know about their mystical origins. The wearing of dreadlocks and the smoking of ‘Ganja’ came from the Naga Ascetics in India who renounced all material possessions and wandered the land, wearing dreadlocks and smoking Ganja from the river Ganga to connect with god. These Naga’s came to Jamaica in the 18th century and the rest is history
